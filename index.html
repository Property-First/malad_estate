Designed for comfort and built for luxury, L&T Malad brings a refined living experience to the heart of Malad, Mumbai. These premium 2, 3 & 4 BHK residences are thoughtfully crafted with smart home features, modern amenities, and spacious layouts that breathe with natural light. Every corner feels intentional, every detail feels elevated.
